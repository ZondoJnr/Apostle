About Thee Apostle Lungi M

Visionary Leader | Author | Chaplain | Coach | Speaker

Biography

Apostle Lungi M is a servant of God called to raise Kingdom leaders and transform nations. With decades of experience in ministry, coaching, and public speaking, she inspires and empowers individuals to live a life of purpose and impact. She is the visionary founder of Kingdom Life Ministries International in Durban, South Africa.

Her work spans church leadership, chaplaincy, authorship, and coaching. She has impacted countless lives, guiding communities in faith, integrity, and excellence.
