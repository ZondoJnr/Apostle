About Pastor Lungi M

Pastor Lungi M is a servant of God, author, chaplain, and commissioner of oaths. She is the visionary founder of Kingdom Life Ministries International based in Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. With a calling to transform lives, she empowers believers to walk in faith, lead with integrity, and impact their communities.
