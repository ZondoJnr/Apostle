6 Months Pre-Marriage Counselling

Our goal: We want couples to soberly decide if they are ready to commit to marriage, helping to curve divorce rates.

  • We teach responsibilities of marriage and your roles within it.
  • We open your eyes to potential marriage pitfalls and what may destroy a union.
  • We verify essential documents:
    • Debts in your name
    • Assets and liabilities
    • Criminal records
    • Customary marriage agreements and cancellations
    • Family backgrounds, cultural and religious information
    • Blood relation checks
    • Children and relationship status verification
    • Dependents verification
  • Referrals to doctors for health checks.
  • Education on types of marriage contracts.
  • Referral to attorneys for legal knowledge of contracts.
  • Discussion of reasons for marriage and red/green flags to help you make an informed decision.

Note: We only conduct official marriage counselling for South African citizens. Non-South Africans are referred to Home Affairs.
